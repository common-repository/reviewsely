Reviewsely is a platform where users can collect, organize and showcase reviews.

== Description ==

Reviewsely(http://reviewsely.com) allows the users who own a business or a website to collect reviews about their business and showcase on their business page or on their Website through widgets and other APIs. Business owners can also imports reviews from social media like Facebook (Facebook Page Reviews), Instagram, Twitter (Twitter Mentions) and Zendesk (Satisfaction Rating from threads).

== Installation ==

1. Goto https://reviewsely.com/register/ and register for an account. A password will be sent to the email you have registered with.
2. Login to your account from this URL. http://reviewsely.com/business-login/
3. Under your wordpress dashboard, Goto "Plugins"->"Add New"->"Upload Plugin" and upload the reviewsely.zip file. This step isn't required if you are installing the plugin from wordpress repository.
4. Once you've installed and activated the plugin, click on "Reviewsely" in the left vertical menu of your wordpress dashboard.
5.  To use
a. Inline Widget. Input this shortcode whereever you want to display the inline widget:
[reviewsely username='YOUR_USERNAME_HERE' type='inline'] 

b.To use grid Widget:  Put this shortcode:
[reviewsely username='YOUR_USERNAME_HERE' type='grid' columns='3']

c. To use Floating Widget: Input your Reviewsely Username against "Reviewselyusername" field, and you are all set. You can configure the size of the widget, color, text and other option.
